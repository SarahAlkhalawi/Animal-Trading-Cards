Wolf

Wolves can sprint from 36-38 miles for short distances.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 2 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 6-8 years
  • Habitat: forests

The wolf has upright ears and it resembles a dog. Telling a wolf apart from a dog can often be difficult, however, the wolf's tail often hangs straight and its eyes are slanted. Wolf tracks are very similar to those of a dog. A wolf's forepaws are bigger than its hindpaws. The wolf's carnassial teeth are longer and larger than those of a dog.
